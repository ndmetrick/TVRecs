TVRecs — Account Deletion

You can delete your TVRecs account and all associated data directly from within the app at any time.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the TVRecs app
  2. Tap the profile icon on the right in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the settings page
  4. Open the Delete Account section
  5. Tap Delete my account
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your username, email, bio, and profile tags
  • All shows on your profile, including tags and descriptions
  • All follows (people you follow and people who follow you)

Deletion is immediate and permanent. This data cannot be recovered after deletion.

Partial data deletion

We do not currently offer partial data deletion. Deleting your account removes all data associated with it.
