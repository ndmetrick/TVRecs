# TVRecs

This is a work-in-progress app for people to recommend TV shows to each other and keep track of shows they want to watch in the future. Users are able to add tags to their profile to describe the type of shows they like, and can add tags to the shows they're recommending to communicate how they would describe the show, things they love about it, and anything they want to warn potential viewers about.

The three types of lists you can have on your profile are your Recommendations (or 'Recs') list, To Watch list (shows you want to keep track of to watch in the future), and Hidden list (shows you don't want to see recommended to you for whatever reason). Shows can only be on one of those lists at a time, and only your Rec list is visible to others. On the main feed page with recommendations from the users you follow, you can toggle on/off seeing shows recommended that you have on your profile on your To Watch and Recs lists (shows in Hidden will never show up in your main feed).

You can search your own profile, your recs, and other users in a variety of ways: filter users for certain tags on their profile, shows they recommend, etc; filter your own profile to just see shows you're currently watching or that you've given certain tags; filter your feed of recommended shows in a variety of ways to help you find shows you're most interested in.

I started this app a number of years ago and stopped when life got busy, but I've come back to it and am getting it back up and running again.
